Fairy tales and stories in Ukrainian for children and teens for free

While Ukraine is bravely defending itself against the barbarity of the Russian army, the little ones suffer the most. If you find yourself in a shelter, basement or under evacuation, take care of the children around you.

Read to them.

Reading books is soothing, it helps you calm the mind, ease the tension and fall asleep. Books can take you to a totally different world – a better world. A world where good always triumphs and evil is punished.

Books for children of different ages have been made available by publishers free of charge. We make them available for you.

All you need to do is just click on the picture and start reading. Read slowly, rhythmically, imitate the voices, make faces and a miracle will happen… I promise…
